Kyndryl Agentic Framework

A design system for evidence-first agentic operations. Every number has a reason, every recommendation has provenance, and every action shows its blast radius before it runs. Aligned to Shidoka (Bridge), Kyndryl's foundation design system.

Sources

This system is compiled from two inputs:

1
Shidoka foundation Brand authority and primary source for tokens — palette, type, space, radii, semantic aliases ($k-color-*, $k-text-*, Opacity.AI.*). Three gray ramps (Dark Stone, Cool Gray, Warm Gray). Type stack: TWK Everett, Roboto, Geist Mono.
2
CMDB Data Quality app Applied patterns and composites from daaron-wright/KyndrylAgenticFramework_cmdb-data-quality. The five archetypes (Posture, Findings & triage, Recommendation & action, Investigation & graph, Conversational & narrative) shape every composite in this system.

Five-layer architecture

01
Foundations — tokens
All in colors_and_type.css. Color (brand, surface, text, semantic, severity, chart, workflow-status), type (display + body + mono + Arabic), space (4→64), radii (2/4/8/16), elevation (rare and subtle), motion (200ms standard ease), iconography (Shidoka sprite at 16/20/24).
02
Primitives
Curated shadcn-style layer plus semantic primitives with opinionated props: SeverityPill, ConfidenceBadge, FreshnessBadge, DeltaIndicator, StatusBadge, SourceAttribution. Every agentic primitive carries a dotted underline, ••• indicator, and a popover with 2–4 actions.
03
Composites
Posture (TrustScoreGauge, KpiTile, KpiGrid, StatusBannerCard), Triage (FindingCard, InspectorDrawer, SignalsPanel), Action (RecommendationCard, ScenarioProjectionCard, ExecutionTimeline), Graph (GraphCanvas, GraphInspector), Chat (ChatMessage, SnapshotCard).
04
Templates
5 page shells plus AppShell: Dashboard, Triage, Investigation, Review queue, Conversation. New pages are composed, not designed.
05
States matrix
Every composite ships loading / empty / filtered-empty / error / stale / unauthorized / executing / success-after-action. Non-negotiable.

Content fundamentals

Voice is operational, precise, quietly authoritative. Sentence case for titles and buttons; uppercase only for eyebrows and severity pills. Numbers are always specific (62% not "most"). No emoji. No exclamation points. Severity is never color-only — always paired with icon or label.
