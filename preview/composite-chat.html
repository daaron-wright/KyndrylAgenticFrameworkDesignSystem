HighlightLink × SnapshotCard pairing
  • Every HighlightLink in the bubble carries a data-key; matching SnapshotCards nest below.
  • Hover/focus on a HighlightLink sets .snap.highlight on the matching card — gradient softens to a 10%-α tint to confirm the link.
